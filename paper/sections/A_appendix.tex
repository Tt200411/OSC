\section{Dataset Integrity}

The Phase-4 preflight required all 12 local CSV files to exist with the expected schema and target column. Solar4 initially produced non-finite losses. The root cause was six missing values in \path{Solar/Site_4_130MW.csv}; these were filled using a minimal local policy and the exact Solar4 missing seed-runs were relaunched. The repair audit stores the file checksum and relaunch details.

\section{Excluded Diagnostics}

Batch-adjusted Lee probes and earlier small-batch diagnostics are not part of the Phase-4 main evidence. They are retained in the repository for hypothesis generation, but every claim in the main paper is based on the matched batch-size-32, six-epoch, three-seed matrix.

\section{Current Evidence Limitations}

The main limitation is that our GELU baseline does not fully reproduce the original Informer Table 2 values. This prevents a claim of reproducing and surpassing the original paper. The present claim is internal and controlled: under the same matched protocol, activation-only replacements often improve over GELU. A second limitation is that factor-bin results are diagnostic rather than causal. They motivate targeted ablations but do not establish a universal volatility rule or a deployable activation router.

\section{Use of AI Tools}

The authors used LLM-based writing tools for drafting support, organization, and copy editing. The experimental design, code execution, result selection, numerical verification, scientific interpretation, and final manuscript responsibility remain with the human authors.
