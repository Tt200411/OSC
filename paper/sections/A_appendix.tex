\section{Dataset Integrity}

The Phase-4 preflight required all 12 local CSV files to exist with the expected schema and target column. Solar4 initially produced non-finite losses. The root cause was six missing values in \path{Solar/Site_4_130MW.csv}; these were filled using a minimal local policy and the exact Solar4 missing seed-runs were relaunched. The repair audit is stored in \path{.aris/phase4_solar4_nanfix_20260521.md}, and the repaired Solar4 SHA256 is \texttt{47c851e6e04642770cd94e76e5b6782bcf4203fb08cf4695a8d08fa6bbd70672}.

\section{Excluded Diagnostics}

Batch-adjusted Lee probes and earlier small-batch diagnostics are not part of the Phase-4 main evidence. They are retained in the repository for hypothesis generation, but every claim in the main paper is based on the matched batch-size-32, six-epoch, three-seed matrix.

\section{Current Evidence Limitations}

The main limitation is that our GELU baseline does not fully reproduce the original Informer Table 2 values. This prevents a claim of reproducing and surpassing the original paper. The present claim is internal and controlled: under the same Phase-4 protocol, activation-only replacements often improve over GELU. A second limitation is that factor-bin results are diagnostic rather than causal. They motivate targeted ablations but do not establish a universal volatility rule or a deployable activation router.
